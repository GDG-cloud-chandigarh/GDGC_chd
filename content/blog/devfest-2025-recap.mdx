DevFest Chandigarh 2025 brought together over 300 developers for a full day of talks across Cloud, AI, Web, and Android tracks.

## Highlights

- Karan Vig's keynote on what's new for Cloud developers
- Priya Nair's deep dive into production ML pipelines with Vertex AI
- A packed hands-on workshop track that ran until the last session

Recordings are up on our [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PLACEHOLDER), and the full photo gallery is linked from the [event page](/events/devfest-chandigarh-2025).

Thank you to everyone who spoke, volunteered, and showed up. See you at the next one.
