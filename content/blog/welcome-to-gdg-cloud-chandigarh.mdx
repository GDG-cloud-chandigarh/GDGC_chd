We're kicking off GDG Cloud Chandigarh to give cloud developers and architects in the tricity area a place to learn, connect, and build together.

## What to expect

- Monthly meetups with talks on real Google Cloud production experience
- Hands-on Cloud Study Jams for certifications like Associate Cloud Engineer
- An annual DevFest with tracks across Cloud, AI, Web, and Android

If you're building on GCP anywhere in Chandigarh, Mohali, or Panchkula, this chapter is for you. [Join us on gdg.community.dev](https://gdg.community.dev/gdg-cloud-chandigarh/) to get notified about the next event.
